Comp 40 - Machine Structure and Assembly Language

Comp 40 is a C-based programming course that challenged me to deeply understand the intimate relationship between hardware and software. To achieve this understanding, I completed 9 major assignments, which ranged from high-level and abstraction heavy to as low a level as working with assembly language and manually packing bits into 64-bit integers. Although this course was by far the most work-intensive class I've ever taken, it was also the most rewarding, as coding at such a high intensity for an entire semester really helped me turn the corner from introductory programming to programming as a professional, with a deep understanding of computer hardware and the way data and code are represented at the machine level.
